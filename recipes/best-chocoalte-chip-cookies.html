Best Chocolate Chip Cookies

Delicious, Nutritious(?), and Easy to Make

Cookies. Fucking COOKIES?! Yea cookies. I know what you're thinking, "I know how to make chocolate chip cookies - why do we need another recipe about chocolate chip cookies.

I guarantee...GUARANTEE....after you make these cookies you'll come to the conclusion that you've never actually made chocolate chip cookies in your life.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup butter, softened
  • 1 cup white sugar
  • 1 cup packed brown sugar
  • 2 eggs
  • 2 teaspoons vanilla extract
  • 1 teaspoon baking soda
  • 2 teaspoons hot water
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 3 cups all-purpose flour
  • 2 cups semisweet chocolate chips
  • 1 cup chopped walnuts

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F
  2. Cream together the butter, white sugar, and brown sugar until smooth. Beat in the eggs one at a time, then stir in the vanilla. Dissolve baking soda in hot water. Add to batter along with salt. Stir in flour, chocolate chips, and nuts. Drop by large spoonfuls onto ungreased pans.
  3. Bake for about 10 minutes in the preheated oven, or until edges are nicely browned.
